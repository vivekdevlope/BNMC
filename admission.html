ADMISSION PROCEDURE
B.N.M. College offers education from Intermediate level to Under-Graduate level for boys & girls students in both Arts & Science Stream. All applicants seeking admission should read the Bulletin of Information/Prospectus issued by the College. The minimum eligibility requirements for admission of various courses shall be specified in that bulletin.

Allocated seats shall be filled in the Colleges for admission purely on the basis of marks/counselling as per the University/State Government rules. Admissions will be closed once the seats are filled.
ADMISSION PROCEDURE
 Request for admission (Arts & Science) will not be entertained without a duly submitted application form. No application for admission will be accepted after the notified date.
 No individual intimation regarding admission will be sent. All notifications regarding admission to various courses will be put up on the College notice board.
 Eligible candidates can apply to the college on a prescribed application which can be availed from the college sales counter along with the prospectus. The Application form should be filled up with all the required details and should be submitted in the college office.
 All notices regarding admission will be displayed on the College Notice Board and College Websites regularly.
 Selected candidates have to appear in counselling and then proceed with admission within the specified date after which their claim shall not be entertained.
 Seats for different caste categories i. e. OBC, EBC, SC & ST are reserved as per the rule of the government. At the time of admission the candidate has to submit the caste certificate compulsorily.
 At the time of admission candidates should choose the subject carefully. After the admission, chosen subjects cannot be changed.
 The original and one attested photocopy of the following documents are to be submitted at the time of admission:

  1. Migration certificate from the institution last attended.
  2. Mark sheet of the examination passed.
  3. School Leaving Certificate / College Leaving Certificate
  4. A separate character certificate from the institution last attended
  5. Two copies of recent unattested stamp size black & white photographs
